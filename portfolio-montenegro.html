Bioclimatic analysis

When it is actually comfortable to be outside

For an inclusive-travel commission — a traveller and her mother, heat-sensitive and with limited mobility — I built an ERA5-Land pipeline computing the felt temperature across coastal and inland Montenegro, to find the hours and the places where being outdoors is comfortable, not just the air temperature.

Accessibility mapping

Beaches that actually clear a flat-entry filter

Alongside the climate analysis, a systematic survey of the coastline: 31 beaches assessed against a flat-entry mobility filter and plotted on an interactive map, with candidate bases, exported to KMZ/KML.
