The verification study

GraphCast vs ERA5 vs station observations

A three-way verification of near-surface wind speed at 25 Antarctic stations. The point: checked against reanalysis alone, the AI forecast looks accurate; checked against the stations themselves, real errors appear that the reanalysis hides. Accepted at EMS2026 (session OSA1.4) and NL Polar Day 2026.
